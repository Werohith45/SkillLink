# SkillLink

SkillLink – Smart Temporary Job Matching Platform

SkillLink is a platform that connects businesses with skilled workers for temporary, part-time, emergency, and short-term jobs.

Many businesses face worker shortages when employees take leave unexpectedly. For example, a hotel may need a replacement chef for a day, a shop may require additional staff during a busy period, or a construction company may need extra workers for a short project.

SkillLink solves this problem by allowing businesses to post job requests and workers to apply for suitable opportunities based on their skills, location, availability, and experience.

The platform includes separate accounts for businesses and workers, transparent salary information, worker and business ratings, and intelligent matching to help both sides find the best opportunities quickly.

Target users:

- Hotels and restaurants
- Retail shops
- Delivery services
- Construction companies
- Event management companies
- Skilled workers
- Students seeking part-time work

Objective:
To reduce unemployment, help businesses fill urgent vacancies, and create a trusted ecosystem for temporary work opportunities.
